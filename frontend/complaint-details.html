Poor Wi-Fi Connectivity in Library North Wing

person

Alex Johnson (Student ID: 29384)

Submitted on Oct 24, 2023 • 10:45 AM

The Wi-Fi signal in the study pods on the 3rd floor of the North Wing has been extremely unstable for the past week. It disconnects every 10-15 minutes, making it impossible to attend online lectures or complete research. I have tried multiple devices (MacBook and iPhone) but the issue persists regardless of the device. Other students in the same area are experiencing similar drops.

support_agent

Campus Support Response

Replied on Oct 25, 2023 • 09:15 AM

Hello Alex, thank you for bringing this to our attention.

Our technical team investigated the Access Point (AP) logs for the Library North Wing 3rd floor. We identified a configuration fault in the load-balancing settings for AP-NW3-04 and AP-NW3-05.

We have pushed a firmware update and adjusted the signal hand-off thresholds. The connectivity should now be stable. Could you please test the connection next time you are in the area and let us know if the issue persists?
